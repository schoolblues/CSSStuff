About Tapir Time

Tapir Time's Mission

Tapir time is a silly website that is design to bring attention to Tapirs. They are endangered and have been endangered for decades. Almost all of their suffering is due to human trifiling, not because they are ill adjusted to their enviorment. Tapir's have lost habitat due to poaching, climate change, road development, cattle farming, rubber plantations, and palm oil plantations. All of their problems are manmade, and because of our collective inconsideration may end up going extinct in the natural world.
